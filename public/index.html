How It Works

1

Upload Your Files

Start by uploading your IFC model file and XER schedule file. Our system accepts standard formats used by major BIM and project management software.

2

Automatic Data Processing

Our system analyzes your files, extracts relevant data, and automatically maps relationships between physical components and schedule activities using WBS codes.

3

Interactive Dashboard

Access your custom dashboard to visualize integrated data including critical path analysis, resource allocations, and risk factors by building component.

4

Edit and Export

Make adjustments to your project data as needed, then export updated files to use in your preferred BIM and scheduling tools.
